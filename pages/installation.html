The Basics

Here are some basic guidelines we follow.
-Wash your hands with soap
and water.
-Look over the skin to identify &
match up where the pieces fit
on your device.
-Water works best for cleaning;
don't use stuff like alcohol or
glass cleaner.
-If the skin sticks to itself, a quick
snap frees it. Yanking or
pulling can stretch it.
-Don't peel up the clear layer; it's
there to protect the skin's artwork.
